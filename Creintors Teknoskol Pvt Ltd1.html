About Us

Creintors Teknosol Pvt Ltd

Creintors Teknosol Private Limited (CTPL) is the precision engineering division of the Creintors Group, focused on designing and manufacturing advanced industrial testing systems and special purpose engineering equipment. Our expertise lies in developing reliable valve testing systems, simulation loops, and specialized industrial rigs that enable industries to test and validate critical components under real operational conditions. With a strong focus on engineering precision, reliability, and performance, CTPL delivers testing solutions that support industries such as energy, heavy engineering, manufacturing, and process industries. Our systems are designed to ensure that valves and components meet strict operational standards before deployment in real world industrial environments. Through innovative engineering and high quality manufacturing, we help industries improve safety, performance validation, and long term operational reliability.
